Role of Parents in Career Planning

When it comes to career planning of your child, you, as a parent, have an important role to play. Your child will look up to you for advice and guidance and therefore, you need to be mentally and financially prepared to help your loved one in this all-important exercise of his/her life.
Read on to know how you can help your child in a successful career planning which will help him/her take the first step towards financial prosperity.

Identifying your child’s interests early

As a parent, you are best positioned to identify the areas of interest of your child. Every child is different and so are their interests. Figuring out areas where your child takes a deep interest goes a long way in identifying the most suitable career option for him/her.
For example, if your child has an active interest in sports, you can zero-in on career prospects in this domain. On the other hand, if your kid is fascinated by gadgets and technology, you can help you explore the career options in this field.
It can be difficult for your child to know where his/her actual interest lies and this is where your role as a parent becomes all the more essential. If your child makes a career in a domain in which he/she has a liking, there are higher chances of success.

Exploring the career scope available

Once you have identified your child's interest, it's time to explore the career scope available and tell your child about the same. The greater the scope, the better are the chances of finding the role that suits your child.
Having said that, there can be instances when the scope is pretty limited. In such a scenario, while you can convey the same, make sure to keep your child motivated. Children can feel disappointed if they find that their interest areas don’t offer ample scope to build a career, and this is when they need support from their parents the most. Your encouragement can do wonders for the confidence of your child.

Evaluating the expenses and being prepared

This is a crucial step in career planning. Once the scope is decided, the next step is to calculate the cost regarding the same. Note that while doing so, you must consider inflation. Also, note that education inflation is rising at a rapid rate than general inflation and therefore, you must be financially ready.
At the same time, it’s vital to make sure that your child's dreams aren’t compromised in your absence. This is where child insurance policies like Aditya Birla Sun Life Insurance Child’s Future Assured Plan help you. Not only it offers assured benefits at key milestones of your child’s life like education and marriage, among others but also has an in-built waiver of premium feature which waives off all the future premiums, in case of an unfortunate event. This plan makes sure your child’s requirements are well-taken care of even in your absence.

Making an informed decision taking over herd mentality

Sometimes, your child may get swayed by peer pressure and opt for a career that the majority is opting for. In such situations, it’s essential for you, as a parent, to help your child make an informed decision and not follow the herd.
Also, you shouldn’t get attracted to a career that’s trending and force your child to pursue it. The aim should be to aid and not dictate.

The final word

During career planning, expose your child to new ideas and perspectives. Also, in an increasingly competitive world starting the process early can help you make changes midway, if required.
