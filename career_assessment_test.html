Career Assessment Test - Its Importance and Effectiveness

When a teacher asked a class of 50 students of 10th grade about what they want to become in future, 20 replied they want to become engineers. 10 of them wanted to become doctors. 8 of them wanted to become actors while 7 of them wanted to be teachers. The remaining 5 were unsure about what they wanted to do in their life.
Exactly after 15 years, 16 people of that class are engineers, 7 are doctors, 8 are entrepreneurs, 10 are teachers, while the rest of them are writers, photographers, and salesmen. None of them went on to become an actor.
What’s the moral of the story? The career paths of people tend to change over the course of time. It may happen that a person who wanted to pursue a different career at a point of time, later came to know that he/she is not happy doing what he/she always wanted to do. It’s very common for people to end up doing something with which they’re not happy.
Today, we have “Career Assessment Tests” which can help your child or student avoid such situations. In many developed countries like the United States of America (USA) and Australia, these tests have gained huge acceptance among the students. However, in India, there’s still a lack of awareness about the career assessment test, its importance, and its effectiveness.

What is a Career Assessment Test?

Selecting the right career for himself/herself can be tough for your child. He/she doesn't know whether the career path he/she wants to take at the present moment will give him/her happiness in the future or not. A career assessment test helps a student in knowing about himself/herself and various career options in depth. These tests generally comprise a series of Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs). Sometimes, the students may also be asked to answer a few subjective type questions.

Importance of career assessment test

The career assessment tests are designed by the psychologists to help the students understand themselves better. By giving these tests, your child will be able to evaluate his/her strengths, weaknesses, passion, perception, interests, and ambitions. By knowing these factors in depth, he/she will be better placed to choose a suitable career for himself or herself.
The answers provided by your child during a career assessment test is evaluated by the experts to know his/her strengths, weaknesses, interests, and is matched with the requirements of different career options. This way, the career counsellor is able to provide better advice to your child about which career is more suitable for him or her.

Below are the 5 reasons why your child must take a career assessment test:
  • To know his/her strengths, weaknesses, passion, and interests
  • To identify the right career for himself/herself and start preparing for it
  • To avoid choosing a wrong career option only to regret later
  • To keep himself/herself motivated after knowing his/her area of strength

Effectiveness of career assessment test

Are career assessment tests really effective? As a parent or teacher, this might be a common question lingering in your mind. The answer to this question is pretty simple. To reach a goal, you need to have a clear strategy or plan in place. You need to know your strengths and weaknesses and choose the right vehicle which can take you to your goal. Similarly, through a career assessment test, a student gets to know himself or herself in depth and hence, is able to create a better strategy regarding his/her career. He or she will be able to approach his/her dreams with more confidence after knowing his/her strengths. And, by knowing his/her weaknesses, he/she can alter or modify his/her strategy so that he/she will have to face least obstacles in reaching his/her career goals.
As per the studies and estimates, a career assessment test plays 30 to 40 percent role in determining the right career choice for a student.
